I. The Argus principle

Named for the watchman who never sleeps.

Argus Panoptes, the hundred-eyed giant of Greek myth, was set to guard what was precious. His attention was permanent; his gaze, total.

We borrowed his name because senior AI talent rarely announces itself. The people who have actually shipped production AI inside European enterprises — who can model a RAG pipeline against a regulator, or stand up an MLOps stack inside a Munich bank — do not sit on job boards. They are observed, mapped, and approached at the right moment. That is the work.

II. For boutique AI consultancies

Your bottleneck is rarely demand.

It is senior consultants who have actually delivered enterprise AI to regulated clients — and who would consider moving.

  • Discretion by default

    No published mandates. No public job boards. Your competitors do not learn what you are hiring for.

  • Senior-only mandate

    We work on roles at lead consultant, principal, and partner level. Junior staffing is a different business.

  • Six-month replacement

    If a placement leaves within six months of start date, we run the search again at no further fee.

  • Mapped, not sourced

    When you brief us, the candidates already exist in our private map of the DACH market. We do not start from zero.

III. For senior AI talent

We don't post jobs. We open doors.

If you are a senior consultant or engineer building production AI inside a European boutique, you do not need another LinkedIn message about a generalist role.

You need a private channel that surfaces only the searches worth your attention — at the firms where the work is actually serious, the partners are accessible, and the equity or carry has been thought through.

V. The method

Three principles. Held without exception.

  1. i.

    Mapping

    We continuously map the senior AI consultant population in DACH — not when you brief us, but months before. Every credible candidate exists in our private record before you ever ask.

  2. ii.

    Filtering

    Each candidate we forward has already been spoken to, vetted on shipped work, and confirmed open to the specific shape of your search. We do not forward speculative names.

  3. iii.

    Closing

    We brief candidates on likely counter-offers before they happen, manage the resignation conversation, and stay engaged through the first month on the job.
