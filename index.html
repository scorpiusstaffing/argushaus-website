The market Why a specialist desk exists

German AI consulting is the largest and most fragmented market in Europe.

Three hundred-plus consultancies. Five hundred more sub-practices inside larger groups. Senior talent rotating between Big Four AI desks, Statworx, Alexander Thamm, Motius, MaibornWolff, Codecentric, Reply's eight sub-brands, dozens of boutiques.

No directory captures it. No LinkedIn search reaches it. The senior consultants who actually ship production AI do not advertise themselves, and the firms that need them rarely know which competitors just lost someone open to a move.

That gap is what we close. Continuously. Independently. In days.
